1. Introduction

Healthwana ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

We operate in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Data Protection Act 2018, and, where applicable, the EU GDPR. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access our website or use our services.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity data: first name, last name.
  • Contact data: email address, telephone number.
  • Health data: information you voluntarily share about your health goals, dietary habits, medical conditions relevant to nutrition, and physical activity — this constitutes special category data under UK GDPR.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type and version, time zone, operating system, and platform.
  • Usage data: information about how you use our website and services.
  • Communications data: any correspondence you send to us via email or through the contact form.

We do not knowingly collect data from or about children under the age of 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our nutrition coaching services.
  • To respond to enquiries and consultation booking requests.
  • To personalise your nutrition programme and track your progress.
  • To send you service-related communications (appointment reminders, plan updates).
  • To send you marketing communications, only where you have opted in.
  • To improve our website, services, and client experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: for health/special category data and marketing communications. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Contract: where processing is necessary to fulfil our service agreement with you.
  • Legitimate interests: for improving our services and website security.
  • Legal obligation: where we must process data to comply with applicable law.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your data with carefully selected trusted partners solely to facilitate our services, including email service providers, video conferencing platforms, cloud storage providers, and anonymised analytics providers.

All third-party processors are bound by data processing agreements and are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data.

6. Data Retention

  • Client health and nutrition data: retained for the duration of the coaching relationship plus 7 years thereafter.
  • Contact form enquiries: retained for 2 years from the date of receipt.
  • Marketing preferences: retained until you withdraw consent.
  • Technical/usage data: retained for 13 months.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, data portability, and the right to object. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@healthwana.com. We will respond within one calendar month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Cookie Policy

We use strictly necessary cookies (essential for operation), analytics cookies (anonymised, consent-only), and preference cookies (consent-only). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

9. Security

We implement HTTPS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, role-based access controls, and regular security reviews. In the event of a data breach posing risk to your rights, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and you directly without undue delay.
