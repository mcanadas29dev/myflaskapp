  • Who is Healthwana designed for?
    Our programmes are designed for adults aged 1865 who want evidence-based guidance  whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced athlete. We tailor everything to your starting point, health history and goals.
  • What makes Healthwana different from a regular dietitian?
    Our founder holds a PhD in Cancer Research and an MPhil in Cell Biology from Cambridge  with deep expertise in metabolic science at the molecular level. This allows us to go far beyond standard dietary advice and address the biological mechanisms behind your results.
  • How long before I see results?
    Measurable changes typically appear within 46 weeks. Sustainable body composition changes (fat loss or muscle gain) usually become clearly visible by week 1012. Biomarker improvements (blood glucose, cholesterol, inflammation) can appear sooner.
  • Do you offer online consultations?
    Yes. All of our consultations and coaching sessions are conducted online via video call, which means we can work with clients anywhere in the world. Our intake process and progress tracking are fully remote.
  • What does a typical plan look like?
    You receive a personalised nutrition protocol that includes caloric and macronutrient targets, meal timing guidance, supplement recommendations (where warranted by evidence), and a food selection framework. Plans are adjusted at regular intervals based on your progress data.
  • How much does it cost?
    Pricing varies depending on the programme and level of support. We discuss this transparently during your initial consultation. We offer a free 20-minute discovery call so there is no pressure to commit before you have all the information you need.
